Block 2 Challenge Proposal: Dungeons and Dragons information App

Purpose

The purpose of this app will be to replace the players handbook into a searchable saveable app.

Audience: D&D players that will ost likely use it on their phone or maybe a tablet

Data Sources

  • D&D 5e API
    •
  • localStorage

Initial Module List

  • My Characters: saved to local storage
  • Create a new character- form
  • roll dice (D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, %die)
  • Spells a comprehensive searchable list and ability to save spells to a character

Wireframes

Styling

I want to give it as medieval a look as possible while still using modern technology.

Colors

I think I will stick with a monotone color scheme of black, red, and white

Typography

I think I will use a serif for small type and an old gothic like font for headers
